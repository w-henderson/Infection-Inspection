Privacy Policy for Infection Inspection

We at Infection Inspection respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide us with when using our mobile game "Infection Inspection".

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information from our users. The only information we store on the local device is the high score and the app version.

The high score is stored locally to allow users to track their progress and improve their gameplay experience. The app version is also stored to help us improve and update the app as needed.

We do not disclose any information to third parties. The information stored on the local device is solely for the user's benefit and will not be shared with anyone else.
